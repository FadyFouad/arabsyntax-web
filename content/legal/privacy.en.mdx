## Introduction

Welcome to ArabSyntax (النحو العربي), an educational app for learning Classical Arabic grammar. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the app. Please read this policy carefully before using the app. By using the app, you agree to the practices described here.

## Information We Collect

We collect the minimum information necessary to operate the service:

- **Anonymous user ID**: We use Firebase Anonymous Authentication to create a unique identifier for you without requiring your name, email address, or any other personal information.
- **Entitlement records**: Your subscription information (plan type and dates) is stored in Firestore and linked only to your anonymous ID.
- **Device information**: Google AdMob collects limited technical information about your device (such as device type and operating system) to serve relevant ads to free-tier users.

We do **not** collect: names, email addresses, phone numbers, precise location, or any other identifying data.

## How We Use Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

- **Providing the service**: Enabling your access to lessons, quizzes, and educational content.
- **Subscription sync**: Verifying your subscription status and restoring access when you change devices or reinstall the app.
- **Serving ads**: Ads are shown via Google AdMob to free-tier users only. Paid subscribers do not see ads.

## Third-Party Services

The app relies on the following third-party services, which may collect their own data:

- **Firebase / Google**: We use Firebase Authentication and Firestore for anonymous identity management and subscription records. This data is subject to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).
- **Google AdMob**: For displaying ads on the free tier.
- **Google Play Billing**: For processing payments on Android devices.
- **Apple App Store**: For processing payments on iOS devices (when available).

## Ads and Consent

The app shows ads via Google AdMob on the free tier. For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), the United Kingdom, and Switzerland, a consent form (UMP form) is displayed on first launch in accordance with GDPR requirements. You can reset your consent preferences at any time from the app's settings.

## Data Retention

- **Anonymous ID**: Retained while you use the app. When you uninstall the app, the ID remains in the database to support subscription restoration. You may request deletion by contacting us.
- **Entitlement records**: Retained for the duration of your subscription and for a reasonable period thereafter.

## Children's Privacy

The app is suitable for use by minors for educational purposes. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from any user, including children. The app does not require registration with a name or email address. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has submitted personal information in any way, please contact us at the address below and we will promptly delete it.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Request deletion of subscription data**: Contact us to request deletion of the entitlement record linked to your anonymous ID.
- **Reset ad consent preferences**: Available from within the app settings (for EEA users).

We respond to all requests within 30 days.

## Security

We apply standard security measures to protect your data, including encryption in transit (TLS) and Firestore security rules for access control. No third party can access your subscription records without authorization.

## International Users

Your data may be processed on servers outside your country through Firebase and Google AdMob services. By using the app, you consent to this processing in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We recommend reviewing this policy periodically.
